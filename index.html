What is Konstellation?

Konstellation is a set of tools dedicated to making artificial intelligence (AI) solutions from lab to production. Our goal is to provide the best way to develop and deploy AI solutions on Kubernetes.

Easy to use

Konstellation covers properly the right process to build, deploy and evolve AI solutions.

Sophisticated UI

Konstellation is giving attention to the user interface that humans must use every day in their jobs to work efficiently and ensuring the reliability of their results.

Deploy at any scale

Konstellation uses Kubernetes so you can easily run into your laptop, server or even into a cloud like aws, Google Cloud, Azure…
